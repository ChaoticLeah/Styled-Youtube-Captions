How to upload your captions to youtube?

Uploading your captions to youtube is pretty simple (NOTE: that once you upload them, dont edit them in the youtube editor otherwise the styling will break). If this is your first time you might want to test it on a private video first.

1. Go to the video you want to upload your captions to in youtube studio.
2. Go to your videos subtitles.
3. add a language.
4. Click the Add button under the subtitles column.
5. Click upload file(with timing), and select your vtt file.
