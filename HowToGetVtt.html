How to get a VTT file from youtube?

Getting a VTT is pretty simple. First edit your subtitles in youtube(or any other software), then if its on youtube download it like this:

1. Go to the video you want to use on youtube.
2. Go to your videos subtitles.
3. click on the 3 dots(Options) of the existing automatic subtitles.
4. Click download
5. Then Click .vtt
